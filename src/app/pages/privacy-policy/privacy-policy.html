Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2026

Thank you for choosing to use Join, a Kanban Board tool developed as a collaborative group project. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Application.

1. Information We Collect

Because this Application is a project developed by a group of developers, we aim to minimize data collection. However, to provide the core functionality of a collaborative Kanban board, we may collect the following information:

    • Account Data: If you create an account, we may collect your username, email address, and password (which is securely encrypted).
      User-Generated Content: Any information, text, files, images, or tasks you create, upload, or move within your Kanban boards, lists, and cards.
      Collaborator Data: Usernames or emails of team members you invite to collaborate on your boards.

    • 2. How We Use Your Information

    We use the collected data strictly for the following purposes:

    • To operate, maintain, and provide the core features of the Kanban Board.
      To allow real-time collaboration and syncing between you and your team members.
      To secure your account and authenticate your logins.
      To fix bugs, improve application performance, and fulfill academic/project evaluation requirements.

    • 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

    We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Your data is only shared in the following limited circumstances:

    • With Other Users: The content you put on shared Kanban boards will be visible to the collaborators you explicitly invite.
      Service Providers: We may use third-party hosting services (e.g., GitHub Pages, Vercel, Render, Heroku, or Firebase) to run the application. These providers handle data strictly in accordance with their own privacy policies.
      Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect the safety and rights of the development team or others.

    • 4. Data Security

    We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and as a project-based application, it may not have the same enterprise-grade security as commercial products.

    5. Data Retention

    We will retain your personal information and Kanban board data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or until the evaluation period of this group project concludes. You may request the deletion of your account and data at any time.

    6. Your Rights (e.g., GDPR)

    Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, including:

    • the right to access the data we hold about you.
      The right to request the correction or deletion of your data.
      The right to withdraw your consent to data processing.

    • To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

    7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
